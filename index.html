Marketing Plan Overview

Phase one focuses entirely on personal insurance — homeowners and auto — building a measurable lead engine before expanding to commercial lines. All four pillars work together to drive awareness, capture intent, and convert visitors into conversations.

01 SEO & AI Search Visibility

Expand the Stocker website with targeted service pages, local area pages, and expert blog content so Google and AI tools consistently surface your agency when Twin Cities homeowners and auto buyers are searching.

02 Meta Ads — Facebook & Instagram

Reach homeowners, recent movers, and auto shoppers across the Twin Cities with targeted campaigns on Meta, including retargeting for warm audiences who have already shown interest in Stocker Agency.

03 Google Search Ads

Capture high-intent buyers already searching for insurance right now. Google carries no special category restrictions for insurance — giving us full targeting flexibility on the terms that matter most.

04 Google Business Profile

Optimize your GBP with photos, service listings, and Google reviews — dominating local map results entirely separate from paid advertising, and building trust before a prospect ever calls.

All four pillars work in concert. SEO and Google Business Profile build long-term organic visibility. Meta and Google Ads generate immediate lead flow. The Website Insurance Assessment converts clicks into advisor conversations.

Meta Ads

Drive quote requests and advisor calls for Stocker Agency from homeowners and small business owners across the Twin Cities metro. Insurance advertising on Meta falls under the Financial Products and Services special ad category, which limits certain targeting options — we work within those guidelines using geographic targeting, broad interest segments, job titles, and custom audiences built from Stocker's own contacts and website traffic.

Three-Campaign Structure
Total Meta: $2,000 – $3,500 / month
Home & Auto Insurance$1,000 – $1,500 / month

Targeting homeowners, renters, and auto insurance shoppers across Bloomington, Minneapolis, St. Paul, Minnetonka, Plymouth, Maple Grove, Eden Prairie, Wayzata, Eagan, Chanhassen, and Lakeville. Audiences include homeowners, people who recently moved, parents with children at home, and people with interests in homeownership and personal finance.

Small Business Owners$1,000 – $1,500 / month

Targeting small business owners who need commercial property, liability, auto, or workers' comp coverage — positioning Stocker as the agent who actually reviews your coverage and helps you explore options. Audiences: small business owners, self-employed individuals, and managing partners in construction, retail, food service, and professional services across the Twin Cities.

Retargeting$500 / month

Re-engage potential leads who visited the Stocker website or engaged with an ad but did not reach out — website visitors in the past 30 days, people who engaged with Stocker's Facebook or Instagram content, and anyone who started the insurance assessment but did not finish.

Objective

Every campaign drives to the Website Insurance Assessment — converting clicks into warm, qualified leads with real context for a follow-up conversation with a Stocker advisor.

What We Measure
  • Assessment completions

Google Search is the most direct path to homeowners and auto buyers already in market. We target the searches that signal intent — "insurance agent near me," "homeowners insurance quote," "car insurance Minneapolis" — and make sure Stocker Agency is the answer.

Why Google Beats Meta for Intent

Meta builds awareness. Google captures demand that already exists. When someone types "home insurance agent near me," they've already decided to shop — we just need to make sure Stocker is the first answer they see.

No Category Restrictions

Unlike Meta's special ad categories, Google allows full keyword targeting, demographic filters, and geographic precision for insurance products — giving us complete flexibility to reach the right audience at the right moment.
