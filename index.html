Marketing Plan Overview

Phase one focuses entirely on personal insurance - homeowners and auto - building a measurable lead engine before expanding to commercial lines. All four pillars work together to drive awareness, capture intent, and convert visitors into conversations.

01 SEO & AI Search Visibility

Give Google and AI more helpful content to work with. We expand the site with individual service pages, local areas served pages, and detailed blog content so Stocker appears for more of the searches your customers are already making.

02 Meta Ads - Facebook & Instagram

Reach homeowners and small business owners across the Twin Cities with targeted campaigns on Meta. We run three campaigns: Home and Auto, Small Business Owners, and Retargeting for people who already engaged with Stocker.

03 Google Search Ads

Capture people already searching for insurance. Google does not carry the same restrictions as Meta for insurance ads, which makes search campaigns a strong complement to this strategy.

04 Google Business Profile

A complete profile means Stocker shows up more consistently in local search and map results, separate from what the ads are doing. We handle photos, reviews, service listings, and ongoing updates.

All four areas work together. SEO and Google Business Profile build long-term visibility. Meta and Google Ads generate lead flow. The Website Insurance Assessment is where all ad traffic goes first, turning clicks into real conversations.

SEO & AI Search Visibility

You already have a strong story, valuable services, and a clear focus on personal service. Now we just need to give Google and AI more helpful content to work with. Since the website is currently fairly small, expanding it will help show the full range of your expertise and allow Stocker to appear for more of the insurance questions and searches your customers are already making.

2026 & Beyond

Simply adding keywords to a page will not be enough. Google and AI increasingly value clear, detailed, accurate, and well-organized content that answers real customer questions, covers important topics in depth, and shows firsthand industry experience. The more helpful and specific Stocker's content is, the easier it will be for search engines and AI tools to understand your expertise and connect your agency with the right audience.

Plan of Attack
01
Individual Service Pages

Create individual service pages for Commercial Property Insurance, General Liability Insurance, Commercial Auto Insurance, Workers' Compensation Insurance, Commercial Umbrella Insurance, and other coverage options. These pages will help Stocker rank for specific searches while giving business owners clear answers about their risks, coverage options, and insurance needs.

02
Local Areas Served Pages

Build individual areas served pages for Minneapolis, St. Paul, Bloomington, Minnetonka, Plymouth, Maple Grove, Woodbury, Lakeville, Blaine, Eagan, Eden Prairie, Wayzata, Orono, and Apple Valley. These pages can help you rank for local searches such as "insurance agency near Eden Prairie" while connecting each community with Stocker's local experience and personalized approach.

03
Blog Content

Publish detailed blog posts that answer urgent, real-world questions. Topics such as "5 Steps to Take When Your Business Outgrows Its Insurance Coverage" build trust, demonstrate real expertise, and help Stocker appear for more questions across Google and AI platforms.

04
Content That Demonstrates Real Expertise

Google and AI systems look for more than just keywords. They value clear answers, useful examples, accurate information, strong organization, and content that fully addresses what customers want to know. By expanding the website around individual services, local communities, and real business problems, we can show Google, AI tools, and potential customers that Stocker is a trusted Minnesota insurance expert, not simply another agency offering quotes.

Your customers are already searching for help with rising premiums, coverage gaps, new employees, business growth, commercial vehicles, and policy renewals. We just need to help Google and AI connect the dots between those business owners and Stocker Agency.

Meta Ads
Objective

Drive quote requests and advisor calls for Stocker Agency from homeowners and small business owners across the Twin Cities metro. Total monthly ad spend: $3,000-$5,000.

What We Measure
  • Assessment completions
  • Quote requests
  • Form submissions
  • Call clicks

Insurance advertising on Meta falls under the Financial Products and Services special ad category, which limits certain targeting options. We work within those guidelines and focus on what is available: geographic targeting, broad interest segments, job titles, and custom audiences built from Stocker's own contacts and website traffic.

Three-Campaign Structure
Total Meta: $2,000 - $3,500 / month
Home & Auto$1,000 - $1,500 / month

We will target homeowners, renters, and auto insurance shoppers across the Twin Cities, including Bloomington, Minneapolis, St. Paul, Minnetonka, Plymouth, Maple Grove, Eden Prairie, Wayzata, Eagan, Chanhassen, and Lakeville. Audiences: homeowners, people who recently moved, parents with kids at home, and people with interests in homeownership and personal finance.

Small Business Owners$1,000 - $1,500 / month

We will target small business owners who need commercial property, liability, auto, or workers' comp coverage. We will position Stocker as the agent who actually reviews your coverage and helps you explore options. Audiences: small business owners, self-employed individuals, and managing partners across the Twin Cities in industries like construction, retail, food service, and professional services.

Retargeting$500 / month

We will retarget potential leads who visited the Stocker website or engaged with an ad but did not reach out. This includes website visitors in the past 30 days, people who engaged with Stocker's Facebook or Instagram content, and anyone who started the insurance assessment but did not finish.
